Super Jump

Do a hop in parallel worlds!

The goal of Super Jump is to let you do a hop in different worlds, where the problem you are trying to solve, or part of it, is well designed, in order to come up with a new brand idea for your field of investigation. Identify through a benchmark some worlds that are somehow connected to your project. Jump into them and use the digital methods to gather images that will inspire your workshop session. Super Jump will allow the working group to take inspirations and have new perspectives about the field of investigation or part of it. You will use these inspirations to stimulate the conversation and generate new ideas during the co-design session.

steps to do

01

Identify and isolate the characteristic that you want to use in your solution:

A

Do this operation manually.

B

Use Marie Kondo or Flock of Birds methods to isolate a cluster or a group in which there is a characteristic you want to explore.

02

Do a benchmark research in order to identify parallel worlds where this characteristic is well designed.

03

Identify the platform from which you want to gather the data and use the Tools to build your dataset. Build a corpus of images for each parallel world that you found in point 2. Be sure to have some metadata.

04

Order your dataset according to the metadata that you believe it is more important and downsize it up to 50 images.

05

Design and print the cards for your co-designing session. On the back side of each card put the metadata that is interesting for the research.

06

Analyze the cards during the co-design session: find behaviour, services, activities, emotions or situations that are well designed and write them on some post-its.

07
