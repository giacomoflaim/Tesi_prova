Open Mic

Listen to the community!

The goal of Open Mic method is to stimulate the conversation during the co-design session through the use of some statements that represent the field of investigation. For each topic there should be between 3 and 5 statements identified that describe the topic itself; the description doesn't have to be a summary of the topic, but it can focus only on some part of it. Thanks to the Digital Methods these Statements can be gathered directly from the web and you can use the metadata presented in the dataset to guide your selection. Open Mic method will allow the working group to have a constant focus on the topic, do some remark about it and meanwhile it will stimulate the conversation and the generation of new ideas.

steps to do
Option A Option B

01

Design your query in order to find the best set of words that represents your investigation.

02

Identify the platform from which you want to gather the data and use the Tools to build your dataset. Be sure to have some metadata.

03

Check manually the consistency of your dataset and eventually clean it from elements that are not related to your research field.

04

Order your dataset according to the metadata that you believe it is more important and downsize it between the top 3-5 statements.

05

Design and print the cards for your co-designing session.

06

During the co-design session use the statements to stimulate the conversation: when the conversation lags use the statement-cards to give a new perspective to the working group.
