Marie Kondo

Group, organize, prioritize and create!

The goal of Marie Kondo method is to deeply examine your dataset in order to come up with a solution or a brand new idea. Group, organize, prioritize and find pattern inside the extracted data so that you can stimulate the conversation inside the work team.
Thanks Digital Methods and extraction tools your dataset will have a lot of metadata, that the working group can use to organize and prioritize the material. In this method all the operation are manual and done according to the metadata; groups and connections are not weighted and based only on similarity. Marie Kondo method will also enable the work team to deeply know the field you designing for.

steps to do

01

Design your query in order to find the best set of words that represents your investigation.

02

Identify the type of content you want to analyze between image, hashtag and text.

03

Identify the platform from which you want to gather the data and use the Tools to build your dataset.

04

Check the consistency of your dataset and eventually clean it from elements that are not related to your research field.

05

If your dataset is really big, order it according to the metadata that you believe it is more important and downsize it. Otherwise skip this step.

06

Design the cards for your co-designing session. Put on the frontside the single elements of your dataset, one for each card. On the back side of each card put the metadata that is interesting for research. If your dataset is the result of many queries, display them somewhere on the card. Then print and cut the cards.

07

Analyze the card during the co-design session: organize, group and prioritize the cards according to the goal of the workshop. Remember to take into account also the metadata related to each card.

08

Find a label which is representative of every group of element you just made.

09

Use this process to start conversations, generating new ideas and combining them into robust solutions.
WHAT NEXT

Flat earth

Focus your attention on one group, identify a obviousness (common behaviour) between all the elements of the group and try to deny it. By doing that you will stimulate further the conversation inside the work team.

Super Jump

Focus your attention on one group and find a common and relevant characteristic inside it. Once you identify it, step into a "parallel world", in which your characteristic plays an important role. Build another dataset that is representative of this parallel world; analyze it and find its strengths. Once you identify them, try to shift this strengths inside your research field.

Statement

Focus your attention on one group and generate between 3 and 5 statements that describe the main characteristic of that group. Than use this statements as starting point to stimulate further the conversation inside the work team.
