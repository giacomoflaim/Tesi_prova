Godsend

Trust the process!

The goal of Godsend is to stimulate the communication inside the working group by throwing a random topic into the conversation, so that you can come up with a brand new idea. Choose between hashtag and images and download the random element. Then ask the working group to force connection between the casual element and the research field. Do not worry if initially the conversation stops for a few seconds, ask the team to generate forced ideas even if they seem to be absurd. Due to Digital Methods the random elements will be always updated based on the trend topics. Godsend will stimulate a lot of the conversation and will allow you to come up with really curious ideas, but it's really important that the facilitator encourages the generation of new ideas.

steps to do

01

Identify the type of content you want to use between image and hashtag.

02

Download the random element: image or hashtag

03

Design the cards for your co-designing session. Put on the frontside the random element.

04

During the co-design session ask the working group to create forced connections between the random element and the research field:

A

If it's a hashtag: the word can be inflect in all the grammatical forms.

B

If it is an image: ask the working group to analyze the image and come up with an interpretation of what appears on it. This interpretation is your new starting point, do some forced connection between it and the research field.

05

Based on the forced connection generate new ideas.

Open Mic

Focus on one idea and generate between 3 and 5 statements that describe the main characteristic of that group. Than use this statements as starting point to stimulate further conversation inside the work team.
